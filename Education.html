Education

Education was added as a pass subject for the first time in 1969 and in the year 2002 honours was introduced. The Department of Education is a full-fledged one with a full time UGC post and in addition staffed by 2 part-time teachers presently. Usually the Department holds seminars with the whole-hearted co-operation of teachers and students. Assignments on topics textual and otherwise have been completed by the honours students under the guidance of the Departmental teachers. Remedial classes besides regular ones are held for the students in necessary papers to assure better understanding of the subject.
