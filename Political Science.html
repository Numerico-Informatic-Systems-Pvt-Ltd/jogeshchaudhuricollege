Political Science

The Department of Political Science started since the inception of the college and developed steadily with Sri Sachindranath Bhattacharya and Sri Kamalendu Ganguly as the first two lecturers. The Department progressed over the time and the subject was then offered as a general course of study.

Honours in Political Science was introduced in the year 2001. Smt. Mousoomi Nandi and Dr. Raj Kumar Kothari nurtured the Department successfully with its newly introduced Honours course and the Department gradually grew from strength to strength. The present faculty includes Smt. Mousoomi Nandi, Ms. Manidipa Mistri, Dr. Samir Kumar Mukherjee, Ms. Pranata Bhattacharya and Ms. Bisari Dey.

Besides the usual class lectures, seminars project work and group discussions are also held which benefit the students immensely. Student- friendly atmosphere and a good teacher-student relationship are the hallmarks of the Department.

Infrastructure:

The central library of the college has sufficient number of Political Sceince and International Relations books for reference of the students. There is a departmental book bank which lends out books to extremely needy students for the whole year. There is a laptop, screen and projector at disposal to enrich the classes audio-visually. Audio-visually equipped classrooms are shared in common.
