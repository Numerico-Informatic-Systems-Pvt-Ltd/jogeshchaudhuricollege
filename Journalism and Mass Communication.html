Journalism and Mass Communication

The Department of  JOURNALISM & MASS COMMUNICATION was set up in the year 2008. The Department started with one teacher  Snehashis Shau, who contributed in numerous ways to build up the Department. Since then the number of students kept on increasing gradually and the Department grew in strength.

Infrastructure:

  • There is a sufficiently equipped media laboratory where practical classes are held.
  • Over the years, a good number of books and journals have been purchased, which are available at the College Library, moreover the Department has a Departmental book bank which lends out books to extremely needy students for the whole year.

  • Students participate in preparing wall magazines on the subject.
