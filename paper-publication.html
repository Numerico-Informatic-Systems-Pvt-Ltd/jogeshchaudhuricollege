Book Publication (By the College)

 

SEMINAR AND PUBLICATION

The College organized seminars in various departments to expand the horizon of learning and equip all with the latest trends in research. As a result of seminars held by the Hindi, English, Commerce , Mathematics and Computer Science Department of the College , the following books with ISBN codes were published:

 

Publication of Department of Hindi

Title: Sampradayikta Aur Samajik Avamulyan ke Daur main Bhisham Sahni, Habib Tanvir aur Safdar Hashmi ka Auchitya

Edited by Sunanda Raychoudhary and Mamata Trivedi 

(ISBN-978-81-924075-2-4)
   
Publication of Department of English

Title: Marginal Writings in English: Bengali and Other Regional Literature. 

Edited by Jaydeep Sarangi and Champa Ghosal.

(ISBN 8172737173)
   
Publication of Department of Commerce

Title: Paradigm Shift in Indian Tax Laws

Edited by Durgadas Bhattacharya & Samir Kumar Sinha 

(ISBN-978-81-924075-0-0)
   
Joint publication of Departments of Mathematics and Computer Science

Title: Quantam Theory and Computer 

Edited by Amit Bhar and Debashis Roy.

(ISBN-978-81-924075-1-7)
