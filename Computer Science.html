Computer Science

Calcutta University extended the affiliation for Honours and General degree courses in Computer Science at Jogesh Chandra College from the academic Session 2001-2002. The first batch passed in both the Honours and General examinations at the university level with fairly satisfactory results. The department can boast of good laboratory with A.C. facility, with 27 computers in an area of around 400 sq. ft. All the computers are networked together, backed with Broadband Internet Service. The department also has a storeroom with an area of around 60 sq. ft. The department has printers, scanner and necessary multimedia kits used for various purposes.
