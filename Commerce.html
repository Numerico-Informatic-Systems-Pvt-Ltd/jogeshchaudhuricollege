Commerce

The Department started functioning from the very first academic session (with effect from 14th July, 1965) of the college. About 450 students in the Pre University (commerce) and B.Com. Pass levels of the Calcutta University were enrolled for study in the first academic session. Initially, the Department had two sections - Day and Evening. The evening section has been closed from the academic session 2000-2001. From the year 1985 the Department has received affiliation for teaching B.Com. Honours in Accounting and Finance of the Calcutta University. Currently both B.Com Honors and B.Com. General in Accounting and Finance courses of the Calcutta University are offered for study.
